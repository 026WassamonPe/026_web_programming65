BANOFFEE PIE

\

This is the best quick banoffee pie ever!! A firm family favourite and super quick to make last minute for unexpected guests :)

Ingredients

  • Digestive biscuits, crushed 250g
  • Butter, melted 100g
  • Carnation Caramel 397g
  • Bananas 2
  • Carton whipping cream, whipped to soft peaks 300ml
  • Grated chocolate to sprinkle 300ml
  • loose-bottomed cake tin 20cm

    • Method

    1. Put the crushed biscuits into a bowl with the melted butter. Mix it together thoroughly, then press the mixture into the base and sides of the tin. Chill for 30 minutes in the fridge.
    2. Simply spoon the caramel from the tin into the base and spread gently to the edges. There’s no need to beat the caramel first.
    3. Remove the pie base from the tin. Slice the bananas and scatter them over the caramel. Top with the softly whipped cream. For the perfect finishing touch, dust with cocoa powder or use grated chocolate to decorate the banoffee pie. Chill until ready to serve.
